## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 📚 DIT PYQ Hub — Full-Stack Academic Resource Platform (PWA)
> Offline-first PWA for browsing, uploading, and downloading university question papers

- **Tech:** React 18, TypeScript, Node.js, Express, Supabase, PostgreSQL, DigitalOcean
- **Key features:** O(1) download counters, offline-first with Workbox, row-level security
- **Impact:** Reliable access during exam season with 99.9% uptime

### ⚖️ Legal Lens — AI-Powered Legal Research Platform (RAG)
> RAG pipeline for statutory research with grounded, citable answers

- **Tech:** Next.js 14, TypeScript, Pinecone, MongoDB, Llama 3.3 70B, LangChain
- **Key features:** Sub-150ms query latency, 92% retrieval accuracy, 85% time saved
- **Impact:** Research time reduced from hours to minutes

### 🦟 Dengue Spot — Real-Time Disease Surveillance Platform
> Community-driven public health platform with live geo-tagged risk mapping

- **Tech:** MongoDB, React, Socket.io, Python, Roboflow, Leaflet.js
- **Key features:** CV-based mosquito breeding site detection, real-time alerts, RBAC
- **Impact:** Early outbreak detection through community participation
